To run program:

Run executable

Input string that corresponds with your box dimensions (one per line, each dimension seperated by an 'x', e.g., 12x22x87)

Press Enter

Output will be:
    the amount of wrapping paper needed for each box (the surface area plus the area of the smallest side for some slack)
    the amount of ribbon needed to wrap the present plus a bow (calculated by the side with the smallest perimeter plus the volume of the box)
